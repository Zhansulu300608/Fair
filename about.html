About us

Make your dreams come true with KAZUNION!

Kazunion Touroperator by Rustar is a rapidly growing tour operator company, part of the international holding Rustar. We have been in the tourism market for more than 15 years.

We have our own host companies in 7 countries (Kazakhstan, Qatar, UAE, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Turkey, Maldives, Mauritius), which ensures a high level of services provided. We also organize tours to such destinations as: Thailand, Malaysia, Vietnam, South Korea, Italy, Slovenia, Czech Republic, Spain, Greece, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Montenegro.
